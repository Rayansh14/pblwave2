Ananya Gupta

Hi there!

I am Ananya Gupta, an art enthusiast, a Netflix addict, an epicurean for Thai cuisine with the new found love for Tiktok.

Initially Colouring, painting and drawing came as a way to relax from my academic routine, but before I realised, I started enjoying being in my own world of doodles and drawings. I strongly believe,

We all have a passion,
BUT
Not everyone is able to discover it;
Not everyone is able to use it;
Not everyone is able to make a difference with it;
Not everyone is able to get recognised by it..

Though if this becomes the case, this world would be a much happier and fulfilling world.

Thanks to my supportive and loving parents, I got this golden platform where I could convert my passion to a platform that could make a difference in someone else’s life as well.. Hence PEBBLEWAVE was born.

This venture retails personalised envelopes, gift tags , wine bags to start with. Part of the proceeds from the sale go towards environmental causes and helping the underprivileged...

“SOME PAINTERS TRANSFORM THE SUN INTO A YELLOW SPOT,
OTHERS TRANSFORM A YELLOW SPOT INTO THE SUN.”
-PABLO PICASSSO.

Our aim at Pebblewave is and will always be to be able to transfrom the yellow spot into the sun... I love my world of art and it will be my honour and privilege to get connected with each one of you.
